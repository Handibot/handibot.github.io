Here are some Early Apps for your FabMo-Enabled Handibot (V2)

Hello new Handibotter! You've gotten one of our first Handibot (V2) Adventure Tools and we look forward to working with you. Not only is the Adventure Edition an all new Handibot, but you are one of the first users of the FabMo digital fabrication platform for running digital fab equipment. We've put together a few Handibot Apps here. Most of these have been installed on your tool. But we will post new App examples as they become available and post updates here as well. Soon we will have a new and more convenient App Store.

TO INSTALL Apps: Click here to download to your device. Then, link device to the tool (AP or Network Mode) and "Install App" from the Dashboard. You will be prompted to browse to the download folder for the App.
