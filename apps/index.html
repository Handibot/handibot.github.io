Here are some Early Apps for your FabMo-Enabled Handibot (V2)

Hello new Handibotter! You've gotten one of our first Handibot (V2) Adventure Tools and we look forward to working with you. Not only is the Adventure Edition an all new Handibot, but you are one of the first users of the FabMo digital fabrication platform for running digital fab equipment.

We've put together a few Handibot Apps here. Most of these have already been installed on your tool. But we will post new App examples as they become available and post updates here as well.

These Apps are the most recent versions from the FabMo App Store -- currently under development but coming to your neighborhood soon!

TO INSTALL Apps: Click here to download App to folder on your device. Then, when linked to your tool (AP or Network Mode), "Install App" from the Dashboard. You will be prompted to browse to the download folder to grab the App.
