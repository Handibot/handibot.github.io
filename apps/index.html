Handibot Apps

Apps for your FabMo-Enabled Handibot

Hello new Handibotter! The current version of the FabMo software will include an automatic install of most of the Handibot Apps listed here, along with default macros. You can re-install them from here if they are lost or corrupted, or to get the latest version of the App.

We will post new App examples as they become available and post updates here as well. Development work for many apps can be found at the FabMo github site.

TO INSTALL Apps: Click here to download an App to a folder on your device. Then, when linked to your tool (AP or Network), click on "Install App" from the Dashboard. You will be prompted to browse to the download folder to grab the App for your Handibot.
